About Soratunagu

Soratunagu (空つなぐ) means "connecting with the sky" in Japanese, symbolizing our mission to connect people with the boundless beauty of traditional Japanese culture.

Based in the historic Saku region, we collaborate with local artisans, chefs, and cultural practitioners to offer authentic experiences that have been passed down through generations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our cultural experiences

Do I need to speak Japanese to participate?

+

No, all our experiences are conducted with English-speaking guides. Our instructors are fluent in English and can provide clear instructions. Some basic Japanese phrases will be taught during the sessions to enhance your cultural experience.

What should I wear to the workshops?

+

We recommend comfortable, casual clothing that you don't mind getting slightly dirty, especially for hands-on workshops like indigo dyeing or pottery. For tea ceremonies and kimono experiences, we provide appropriate attire or guidance on what to wear.

Can I take home what I make?

+

Yes! All items you create during our workshops are yours to keep. For pottery workshops, we can arrange shipping if your creation needs to be fired in a kiln after your session. Shipping fees may apply for international deliveries.

Are the experiences suitable for children?

+

Most of our experiences are suitable for children aged 8 and above when accompanied by an adult. We offer special family-friendly sessions for some workshops. Please check the specific experience details or contact us for more information about age suitability.

What is your cancellation policy?

+

We understand that plans can change. Cancellations made 48 hours or more before your scheduled experience will receive a full refund. Cancellations within 48 hours are eligible for a 50% refund or the option to reschedule for another date (subject to availability).

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions for food experiences?

+

Yes, we can accommodate most dietary restrictions with advance notice. Please inform us of any allergies or dietary requirements when booking, and we'll do our best to adapt the experience accordingly. Vegetarian and vegan options are available for most culinary workshops.
